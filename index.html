Features

Everything You Need to Rank Up

Background Recording

Record your screen while playing — no performance impact. The recorder captures your gameplay silently in the background.

Llama AI Analysis

Meta's open-source Llama model analyzes your gameplay frame-by-frame, identifying patterns, mistakes, and opportunities.

Post-Match Review

Get instant tips after each match — no waiting. Analysis covers aim, movement, utility usage, and game sense.

Performance Tracking

Track your improvement over time with detailed charts and metrics. See which areas improve and where you still need work.

Agent-Specific Tips

Get coaching tailored to your agent pick — whether you're an entry fragger on Jett or a sentinel holding site on Killjoy.

100% Private
